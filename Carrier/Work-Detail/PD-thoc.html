Product Designer

The House of Commons
Remote Full-time Posted Today
Apply now

The House of Commons is looking for an experienced Product Designer to shape the future of decentralized media. In this role, you'll collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive design for cutting-edge products in the fast-evolving world of digital media.

What You'll Do

  • Collaborate within a dynamic team to design new products and features for web and mobile, while enhancing and optimizing existing user experiences.
  • Partner closely with Product Managers and Engineers throughout the product lifecycle, from ideation through production.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs that communicate your design solutions.

Our Stack

  • Figma, Adobe Creative Suite
  • Prototyping tools (Principle, Framer)
  • Basic understanding of HTML/CSS
  • Collaboration tools (Notion, Slack)

Benefits

  • Competitive salary package
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Annual learning budget
  • Flexible working hours
  • Remote work options
